RingSupplies.com

Ring supplies is a great website to find ring blanks, lathe mandrels, and stones.

Patrick Adair supplies is another great place to buy stones and ring blanks. You can also get CA glue and Resin.
Another cool thing they do is sell complete ring kits that include ring blanks, inlays, and stones/powders needed to make a specific ring.
