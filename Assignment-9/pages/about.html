Why Inlay Rings?

I chose to teach people how to make inlay rings because my dad used to make them.
He started making them because he didn't see a lot of inlay rings being sold at jewelry
stores and they looked relativly easy to make. What started as a hobby turned into a business, and he taught me how to make them too.
I am not a very crafty person and this is about the only craft-like thing I know how to make, so I thought it would be a cool instructable.
It's also something my dad and I both like to do so I thought it would be fun to talk about.
